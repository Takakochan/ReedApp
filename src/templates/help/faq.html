Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ReedManage

General

What is ReedManage?

ReedManage is a reed tracking application designed for double reed musicians (oboe, bassoon, and related instruments). It helps you log, track, and analyze your reed-making progress.

What instruments are supported?

All double reed instruments: oboe, bassoon, oboe d'amore, English horn, contrabassoon, and historical instruments.

Is ReedManage free?

Yes! ReedManage is currently free to use. We plan to introduce optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

Do I need to track every parameter?

No. Start with the basics (cane brand, quality rating) and add more parameters as you get comfortable. The app is flexible - track only what matters to you.

Getting Started

How do I add my first reed?

Click "Add Reed" in the sidebar and fill in the fields. Don't worry about completing every field - just add what you know. See our Quick Start Guide for detailed instructions.

What's the difference between single and batch entry?

Single Entry: Add one reed at a time with all available fields.
Batch Entry: Add multiple reeds at once with customizable fields - perfect for logging several reeds from one reed-making session.

Can I customize which fields I see?

Yes! In batch entry mode, use the checkboxes at the top to select which parameters to display.

Where can I learn more about reed parameters?

Check our Quick Start Guide for detailed technical explanations of all parameters including diameter, hardness, flexibility, density, and more.

Features

What's the weather feature for?

Weather (temperature and humidity) significantly affects reed performance. ReedManage automatically fetches weather data when you add a reed to help you understand environmental impacts.

Where can I see statistics about my reeds?

Go to your Account page and click "View Statistics" to see quality trends, best performing brands, correlations between parameters, and ML predictions.

How do I export my data?

Visit your Account page and find the "Download My Data" section. Choose from CSV (spreadsheets), Excel (formatted), or JSON (developers) formats.

Can I use ReedManage on my phone?

Yes! ReedManage is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Open it in your mobile browser and select "Add to Home Screen" (iOS) or "Install App" (Android) for an app-like experience.

Does it work offline?

Basic functionality works offline thanks to PWA technology. Your data syncs when you're back online.

Data & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use encrypted passwords, rate limiting, HTTPS encryption, and regular security updates. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact us to request account deletion. We'll permanently remove all your data within 30 days.

Do you share my data with third parties?

By default: NO. Your reed data is completely private and belongs to you.

Optional Data Contribution Program: You can choose to share anonymized, aggregated data to help improve the reed-making industry. In exchange, you get Premium features FREE (a $120/year value).

Participation is 100% optional, opt-in only, and you can revoke permission anytime. Learn more about our Privacy Policy.

Who can see my reed data?

FREE users: Only you. Your data is completely private.

PREMIUM users: You can see how your stats compare to anonymized community averages (e.g., "Your average quality: 3.8 vs. community: 3.4"). Individual data remains private.

Data Contributors: Your anonymized data contributes to industry insights. We never share identifiable information - only aggregated statistics like "65% of oboists prefer medium hardness."

What's the Data Contribution Program?

A voluntary program where you share anonymized reed data and receive Premium features FREE in exchange.

You get: Premium features ($9.99/month value), community insights, quarterly reports, contributor badge.

We share (anonymized only): Statistical trends with cane manufacturers to help improve products.

We NEVER share: Your name, email, individual reeds, or identifiable information.

What are Premium features?

Premium ($9.99/month or FREE for Data Contributors):

  • Compare your stats vs. community averages
  • Advanced ML-powered analytics and predictions
  • See top-rated brands and popular choices
  • Custom correlation analysis
  • Priority support and early feature access

Troubleshooting

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Contact us and we'll help you reset your password. (Automated password reset coming soon!)

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try: clearing your browser cache, updating your browser, disabling extensions, or using a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge). If problems persist, contact us with details.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Thank you! Use our contact form to report bugs. Include what you were doing, what you expected, and what actually happened.

Can I edit or delete a reed after adding it?

Yes! Go to "All Reeds" in the sidebar to view, edit, or delete any reed in your collection.

My cane brand isn't in the list. What do I do?

Contact us and we'll add it to the database immediately.
